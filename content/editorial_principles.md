## Story-first promise

This edition places the full story before any commentary. The first read is uninterrupted.

## Determinism and pins

P1. The story text is pinned from a private ingest release (canonical.txt + sha256 + provenance).

P2. The analysis tables are generated deterministically from pinned inputs in a public MIT apparatus repo (linked from NEKpress.ca).

P3. The paperback build pins inputs and records receipts so the final PDF can be reproduced.

## No filler

Every section after the story earns its pages by doing one of three jobs:

J1. Provide a clear reread tool (ledger, cards, keyed notes).

J2. Provide context that changes what a reread can notice (medicine, property, labor, publication).

J3. Provide a compact reference that supports discussion (glossary, guide).

## Editorial stance

The apparatus does not claim to settle interpretation. It aims to make interpretation explicit, testable, and discussable.
